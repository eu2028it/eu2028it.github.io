2028 Italian EU Council Presidency

Every six months, when an EU member state holds the EU Council Presidency, a website is created at
eu[year].[country TLD]. Most countries register their domain years in advance. Unfortunately, for their
2028 Presidency, Italy forgot, so we decided to do it for them. We bought the domain for $8.14, and set up
a website. We're pretty sure this makes us the king of Europe or something, but we're not totally sure.
